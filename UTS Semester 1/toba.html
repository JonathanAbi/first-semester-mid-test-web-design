Lake Toba

Lake Toba is an amazing natural wonder located on the island of Sumatra. The natural beauty of Tao Toba offers the charm of green mountains spoil the eyes, clean air that is soothing, the vast expanse of lake water that refreshes the mind, and other wonders that can only be found when setting foot there. Lake Toba is a world-class geopark-based tourism destination that offers a diversity of natural, cultural and historical based attractions.
